# Critical Systems Risk Audit — One-Pager

## Who it is for

Founders, CTOs, Heads of Engineering and product leaders responsible for business-critical systems in fintech, Web3, SaaS, AI, embedded-to-cloud or regulated environments.

## Problem

The team can ship, but leadership is not fully confident about release risk, platform reliability, observability, incident readiness, QA coverage, payment/transaction edge cases or compliance evidence.

## Audit question

Where can this system fail in production, delivery, customer trust, audit or money-flow integrity — and what should be fixed first?

## Assessment areas

- Architecture and ownership boundaries
- CI/CD and release gates
- QA strategy and automation coverage
- Platform reliability and cloud infrastructure
- Observability and incident readiness
- Security and compliance evidence
- Payment / transaction / wallet / fee risks
- AI agent / LLM workflow risks
- Embedded-to-cloud boundary risks
- Team process and delivery risk

## Deliverables

- Top risk map
- Failure-mode notes
- Prioritized remediation backlog
- 30/60/90 action plan
- Executive summary

## Engagement formats

- Snapshot review
- Standard audit
- Deep audit with workshops
- Retainer / fractional advisory
- Technical due diligence

## Positioning line

Critical systems do not fail only in code. They fail across boundaries — architecture, infrastructure, release process, QA, observability, data, operations and ownership.
